The Best Movies According to Tchatua

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Designated Survivor

Designated Survivor" is not a movie, but a political thriller drama television series created by David Guggenheim. It premiered on ABC and later aired on Netflix.

The show follows Tom Kirkman (played by Kiefer Sutherland), a low-level cabinet member who becomes President of the United States after a catastrophic attack during the State of the Union kills everyone above him in the presidential line of succession.

A Few Good Men

This is one of the movies, I have watched many many times. First few times, Tom Cruise seemed to be giving an ordinary performance; probably my shortcoming that I was not able to understand his character. But, then I realized that he was playing exactly the character he was supposed to portray and he did it in a impeccable manner - wow!!!! He was just second to Jack Nicholson.

The Book of Eli

The Book of Eli" is a generally well-regarded post-apocalyptic film, particularly praised for its visuals, acting, and unique take on the genre. While not universally acclaimed, it's considered a visually stunning and thought-provoking movie with a memorable performance from Denzel Washington.
